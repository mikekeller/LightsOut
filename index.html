Welcome

Lights Out is an HTML5 game. It is based on Tiger Toys electronic Lights Out game. It is written in HTML5 canvas and JavaScript.

Game Objective:
You are presented with a board of lights. When a light is lit (on), it appears yellow. When a new game is initiated, a random setting of lights will be lit. Clicking on a light (whether lit or not) will toggle its state as well as the state of the lights above and below and to the right and left of it. Your goal is to turn-off all the lights.

To Play:
1. Select the level difficulty. (1 is easiest 5 most difficult)
2. Press the New button to initialize the game and start the timer.
3. Click lights turned on until all lights are out.

Each move you make is reported in Moves. When all lights are out the game so informs you and the Elapsed timer is stopped. At any time after pressing New button you can restart the game from the beginning by pressing the Replay Game button. You can press See Solution to have the game step through and push the buttons in order to turn off all the lights.
